About the Event

Code for Change is a student-led hackathon focused on solving local challenges like food insecurity, transportation access, and literacy. Over 24 hours, teams of 3–5 students will brainstorm, build, and present digital tools that make a difference. Whether you're into design, coding, or problem-solving, this event gives you the platform to collaborate and innovate. No prior hackathon experience is needed—just a desire to learn and make an impact.

What to Expect

A weekend of impact, innovation, and collaboration.

Real-world Challenges
Choose from local topics like food justice, education, or accessibility.
Mentorship
Get feedback from experienced developers, designers, and social impact leaders.
Beginner Friendly
Workshops, team support, and resources for first-time hackers.

Event Schedule

Friday Evening – Spark Session

Meet your fellow changemakers, form your teams, and get inspired by real stories from your community.

  • Community challenge briefs revealed
  • Mini team-forming games
  • Lightning talks by local organizers & developers

Saturday Morning – Kickoff & Code Sprint

Fuel up with breakfast and hit the ground running—start coding with purpose!

  • Morning check-in and mentor welcome
  • Teams brainstorm solutions
  • Development begins: design, prototype, test

Saturday Afternoon – Mentor Mayhem

Need help? Mentors are on deck for debugging, feedback, and advice.

  • Dedicated "ask a mentor" hours
  • Optional workshops (UX for good, Git crash course)
  • Team progress check-in

Saturday Evening – Code & Recharge

Keep the momentum going—or take a break to recharge your brain.

  • Dinner & soft music lounge
  • Optional community activity (guided journaling, walk & talk)
  • Late-night debug zone (with snacks!)

Sunday – Demo Day & Celebration

Time to share what you built and see what others have created for change.

  • Submit your project by noon
  • Live demos: 5 minutes per team
  • Judging & audience choice voting
  • Awards + closing remarks
  • Celebration & team photos
